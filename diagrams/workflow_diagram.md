# Parking Permit System Workflow

1. **Vehicle Registration**
   - Users register their vehicles in the system.

2. **Permit Request**
   - After registration, users can request permits.

3. **Zone Check**
   - The system verifies if the vehicle is eligible for the requested zone.

4. **Permit Approval**
   - Once eligibility is verified, permits are approved or denied.

5. **Parking Verification**
   - The system verifies whether the vehicle is parked in the approved zone or not.
